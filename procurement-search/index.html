Can I filter tenders by budget above $1 million?

+

Yes. You can set any minimum budget threshold. We automatically convert all currencies to your preferred one (USD, EUR, UAH, GBP) so you can compare tenders across countries with accurate, up-to-date exchange rates.

How does auto-translation work?

+

We use AI-powered translation to convert tender descriptions, requirements, and attached documents into your preferred language. This means a tender published in Polish, German, or French is instantly readable in English or Ukrainian. Translation quality is optimized for procurement terminology.

How often is the data updated?

+

We sync with all platforms multiple times per day. Most new tenders appear in TenderScope within hours of being published. Daily alert digests are sent every morning at 8:00 AM in your timezone.
